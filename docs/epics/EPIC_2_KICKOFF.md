# Epic 2

## Educational Intelligence

Version

v0.5.x Development

Status

ACTIVE

---

# Vision

Transform Kwalitec from a curriculum platform into an Educational Intelligence platform capable of understanding a student's educational state and recommending the highest-value next learning action.

---

# Mission

Build a Digital Twin that continuously evolves through evidence and becomes the authoritative educational model of every student.

---

# Primary Objective

Represent learning.

Not study.

Not activity.

Not motivation.

Learning.

---

# Guiding Question

Every capability developed during Epic 2 should help answer:

> What is the highest-value thing this student should do next?

---

# Educational Intelligence Domains

The Digital Twin will eventually contain:

Knowledge

Memory

Behaviour

Performance

Confidence

Readiness

Decision State

---

Knowledge and Memory have already been established.

Epic 2 focuses on completing the remaining educational domains and connecting them into a coherent reasoning system.

# Architecture Principles

The Digital Twin remains the single source of truth for educational state.

Services consume the Twin.

They do not create educational state.

---

# Success Criteria

Epic 2 succeeds when:

- every educational state is evidence driven;

- recommendations become explainable;

- missions become adaptive;

- readiness becomes measurable;

- every recommendation can be traced back to evidence.

---

# Non-Goals

Epic 2 is not about:

- UI redesign

- gamification

- analytics dashboards

- notifications

- social features

Those belong to future Epics.

# Closing Statement

Epic 1 taught Kwalitec what the student needs to learn.

Epic 2 teaches Kwalitec how to understand the student.

Only when both are complete can the platform make genuinely intelligent educational decisions.
