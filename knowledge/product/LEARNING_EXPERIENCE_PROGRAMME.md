## 1. Why This Programme Exists

Educational Integrity Programme is **COMPLETE**.  
Educational Governance is **CERTIFIED**.  
The educational model is considered **stable**.

The Blind Internal Alpha Review identified a critical product gap that governance and integrity alone cannot close:

> Students complete Today’s Mission but there is **no structured educational closure** — and therefore **no educational bridge** between mission, study, performance, evidence, and tomorrow’s guidance.

Constitutional states, Evidence hierarchy, and lifecycle architecture already define *what may lawfully happen*. This Programme defines *what the student experiences so that those lawful happenings actually occur*.

Without this loop:

- Today’s Mission remains a timer with a checklist;
- Study Progress may advance while Understanding Evidence never arrives;
- Estimated Knowledge, Estimated Readiness, and meaningful Educational Guidance remain permanently empty or thin;
- Daily use collapses into “I can do this in a spreadsheet.”

With this loop:

- Each study day produces honest coverage *and* a chance for qualifying Educational Evidence;
- Closure is educational, not theatrical;
- Tomorrow’s Mission is regenerated from updated educational state, not from hope.

## 2. The Missing Product Loop

### 2.1 Blind Review product evidence (summary)

Treated as product evidence, not architecture:

| Observation | Product consequence |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Mission lists minutes for Core Reading / questions / reflection but contains no material and no capture of results | Activity without Observation |
| “Mark Session Complete” only greens checkboxes and bumps coverage | Completion without Evidence |
| Nowhere to log accuracy, scores, or how studying went | Understanding spine never starts |
| Analytics wait forever for data the product never collects | Cold-start silence becomes product failure theatre |
| Recommendations say “study the next topic” without personal performance history | Guidance without Evidence succession |

### 2.2 The broken chain today

```
Open Today's Mission
        ↓
Study elsewhere (outside Kwalitec)
        ↓
Mark Session Complete
        ↓
Study Progress may advance
        ↓
✗ No structured practice outcome
✗ No reflection capture
✗ No Educational Evidence of understanding
✗ No lawful estimate movement
✗ Tomorrow looks like today with a for-loop
```

### 2.3 The intended educational chain (already constitutional)

From Lifecycle Architecture §1.1 and Evidence Model §1.2:

```
Educational Activity
        ↓
Educational Observation
        ↓
Educational Evidence          ← only when authorised
        ↓
Educational State Updates     ← ownership rules
        ↓
Educational Guidance          ← tomorrow’s mission / advice
```

LXP-001 productises this chain as a **daily student journey**, without inventing scoring maths or Twin redesign.

### 2.4 Two spines that must both complete

| Spine | Question | Version 1 daily outcome |
|-------|----------|-------------------------|
| **Coverage** | What have I studied / what am I learning now? | Mission work may advance Study Progress; Current Learning Topic may advance |
| **Understanding** | What observations support claims about how well I know this? | At least one qualifying practice or soft observation path may produce Evidence |

Mission Completion may connect both spines — coverage advancement on the first; opportunity to gather Evidence on the second — **never** automatic mastery (EL-004; Constitution IV.14).

## 3. Version 1 Learning Journey

### 3.1 Journey bounds

| Bound | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Start** | Student opens Today’s Mission for the active Study Plan under Learning Mode |
| **End** | Tomorrow’s Mission (or next day’s session commitment) is generated from updated educational state |

### 3.2 Journey narrative (one sitting)

1. Student opens Today’s Mission and understands **what to study today and why** (Learning Mode / Current Learning Topic).  
2. Student undertakes **study activities** for that topic (reading / Core Reading engagement — may be external material).  
3. Student undertakes **question practice** with recorded outcomes (attribution to topic required).  
4. Student **completes the session** as a bounded commitment.  
5. Student briefly **reflects** on how the session went.  
6. System records lawful **Educational Evidence** (and Observations) from what was gathered.  
7. Student receives plain-language **feedback**: what advanced, what did not claim mastery, what happens next.  
8. System prepares **tomorrow’s guidance** — next Mission from updated Current Learning Topic / Study Progress, with honest narration of what changed.

### 3.3 Journey principle

> **Reduce decisions. Increase learning.**  
> Each day answers three student questions before the student leaves:  
> (1) What did I do? (2) What did we learn about how I’m doing? (3) What do I do tomorrow?

If any of those three cannot be answered honestly, the loop is incomplete.

## 4. Step Catalogue

For each step: Purpose · Student interaction · Educational meaning · Educational states affected · Evidence created · What the student should feel.

Evidence references use Evidence Model catalogue IDs (EV-01…EV-12) where applicable. Quality Levels follow EIP-002-DESIGN §4. No numerical scoring is defined here.

---

### Step 1 — Beginning of Study Session

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Convert educational state into one clear daily commitment so the student does not renegotiate the syllabus every morning (EL-003). |
| **Student interaction** | Open Today’s Mission (or “Start today’s session”). See: topic title; short plain-language why (Learning Mode / next incomplete unit); the session’s study tasks; how to begin. Optional: confirm start / start timer — **without implying mastery**. |
| **Educational meaning** | Mission is a commitment, not an assessment certificate. Current Learning Topic under Learning Mode is the authorised focus (EL-002, EL-009). |
| **Educational states affected** | Mission lifecycle → in progress (or equivalent). Current Learning Topic presented, not rewritten by recommendations. Study Progress / Estimates unchanged by opening alone. |
| **Evidence created** | None by opening alone (navigation is not Educational Evidence — Constitution V §2). Optional Level 0/1 session-start Observation (administrative / engagement) may be recorded as history, not understanding Evidence. |
| **What the student should feel** | Clarity and calm: “I know exactly what I’m doing today, and why it’s this topic.” Not lectured; not oversold as intelligence theatre. |

**Version 1 truthfulness constraint:** The “why” answers syllabus continuity and Study Progress — e.g. next unit on the official path — not invented adaptive interruption (Blind Review: next-topic honesty is acceptable; pretending intelligence when none was measured is not).

---

### Step 2 — Study Activities

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Honour first-pass learning of the Current Learning Topic: exposure to official / syllabus-aligned study material (Core Reading or equivalent). |
| **Student interaction** | Follow mission study tasks for the focal topic. Version 1 **may** rely on external study materials (ActEd, Core Reading, notes) while Kwalitec remains the commitment and recording surface. Student marks or confirms study engagement for the unit when asked (e.g. “I studied this material”). Optional: record approximate study duration when attributable and honest. |
| **Educational meaning** | Study is exposure and coverage work — **not** demonstration of understanding (Constitution II §1.2; EV-01, EV-12). |
| **Educational states affected** | May contribute toward lawful Study Progress for the unit when the product treats study obligation completion as coverage (EL-001) — typically at Session Completion, not mid-scroll. Does **not** write Estimated Knowledge / Mastery. |
| **Evidence created** | EV-01 (Reading / engagement) as Level 1 engagement Observation when reading contact is recorded; EV-10 (Time spent) only as weak engagement / pace signal — **never sole basis for knowledge claims**; EV-12 may update Study Progress as declaration, not understanding Evidence. |
| **What the student should feel** | Supported, not empty-handed: “I’m studying with a plan,” even if content lives outside the app. The app must not pretend it contains material it does not. |

**Blind Review bridge:** Version 1 does **not** require a full in-app question bank or Core Reading corpus to close the loop. It **does** require that study activity is a named step with truthful capture — not an invisible “go away and come back.”

---

### Step 3 — Question Practice

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Produce observable performance episodes that may lawfully enter Educational Evidence and later support Estimated Knowledge / Readiness (Constitution IV.13; EV-03, EV-04). |
| **Student interaction** | Undertake question practice attributed to the mission topic. Version 1 minimum: student can **record structured practice results** for the session’s topic — at least counts attempted and correct (or equivalent accuracy outcome), optionally difficulty / source note. Prefer in-app practice when available; **external practice with attributed outcome logging is educationally lawful** for Version 1 if outcomes are recorded as student-attributed structured results. |
| **Educational meaning** | Attempt ≠ Success ≠ Mastery. Practice creates Observation / Evidence opportunity; it does not certificate the topic (EL-004, EL-005). |
| **Educational states affected** | Study Attempt / Observation created. Estimated Knowledge / Mastery **may** update later only via authorised Evidence → Twin path — never by this Programme inventing maths. Study Progress does **not** advance from a lone attempt (Evidence Model EV-03). |
| **Evidence created** | EV-03 Question Attempts (with outcome); EV-04 Question Scores / measured accuracy when recorded. Typical Quality Level 2–3 (Practice / Assessment) depending on key reliability. This is the **primary Version 1 understanding-spine entry point**. |
| **What the student should feel** | Useful and measured: “The app now knows something about how I did today — not just that I showed up.” |

**Hard product rule:** If the student cannot record how practice went, Step 3 is absent and LXP-001 has failed — regardless of how polished Mission copy is (Blind Review killer gap).

---

### Step 4 — Session Completion

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Close the bounded daily commitment honestly: honour finished work; update only states completion lawfully supports (EL-004). |
| **Student interaction** | Confirm “Session complete” / “Mission complete” after study and (preferably) practice capture. Explicit confirmation required — silent auto-mastery forbidden. |
| **Educational meaning** | Completion answers “I finished today’s commitment.” Completion ≠ Mastery. Completion ≠ Estimated Knowledge write (EV-02). |
| **Educational states affected** | Mission lifecycle → completed. Study Progress for covered unit(s) **may** become completed studying when the mission was coverage work for that unit (EL-001, EL-004). Current Learning Topic may advance **only via** Study Progress rules (EL-002) — not via mastery labels. Estimated Knowledge / Mastery unchanged by completion alone. |
| **Evidence created** | EV-02 alone: **no** understanding Evidence. Completion may **accompany** distinct Evidence items already gathered in Steps 2–3 and 5. |
| **What the student should feel** | Relieved and truthful: “I’ve closed today properly.” Never: “I’ve mastered this because I ticked a box.” |

---

### Step 5 — Session Reflection

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Capture the student’s considered self-view after study/practice — metacognition that aids coaching honesty and soft calibration (EV-08, EV-09; Constitution IV.10). |
| **Student interaction** | Short, plain prompts after completion (or as final mission task). Version 1 minimum set (choose concise instruments, not essays): (a) How clear do you feel on today’s topic? (b) Optional felt confidence (low / medium / high or equivalent); (c) Optional one-line: what was hardest. Must be skippable only with disclosure that skipping delays soft guidance — never force theatre. |
| **Educational meaning** | Reflection is Rank-D / Level 1 soft signal — valuable for honesty, **never sole path** to strong knowledge or high Estimated Mastery language (Constitution V §4; Evidence Model Level 1). |
| **Educational states affected** | Soft Observation recorded. May cautiously inform readiness/workload narratives. Must **not** write Study Progress solely from reflection. Must **not** alone drive Twin understanding estimates as if assessment occurred. |
| **Evidence created** | EV-08 Reflection; EV-09 Self-reported confidence (soft only). |
| **What the student should feel** | Heard, not interrogated: “Someone asked how it went — and took the answer seriously without pretending it was an exam score.” |

**Blind Review bridge:** Today’s Mission already lists “Reflect and review” as minutes; LXP-001 makes reflection a **captured product step**, not an unpaid suggestion.

---

### Step 6 — Educational Evidence Produced

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Materialise the succession Activity → Observation → (authorised) Educational Evidence so understanding claims can ever become lawful (Lifecycle §1; EL-005). |
| **Student interaction** | Student does not manage “Evidence” jargon. Student sees confirmation that practice results and (if any) reflection were saved, in plain language (“Practice results recorded for 2.1”). |
| **Educational meaning** | Evidence is observed history, not a recommendation written backwards into the past (Evidence Model §2). Quality Levels determine claim lawfulness; accumulation still required for strong mastery speech (Constitution V §5). |
| **Educational states affected** | Educational Evidence store accumulates authorised items from the session. Twin-owned Estimated Knowledge / Mastery **may** succeed only through existing authorised estimation paths when Evidence warrants — **this Programme does not specify estimation formulae**. |
| **Evidence created** | Session packet, educationally speaking: typically EV-03/EV-04 (required for understanding spine), optional EV-01/EV-10, optional EV-08/EV-09. EV-02 never substitutes for EV-03/EV-04. |
| **What the student should feel** | Trust accruing: “What I did today will show up tomorrow in how the app guides me.” |

**Silence rule:** If no qualifying practice outcome was recorded, the product must **not invent** Evidence, Estimated Knowledge movement, or “you’re improving” rhetoric. Prefer cold-start honesty (Constitution III §§2–3).

---

### Step 7 — Student Feedback

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Close the session emotionally and cognitively with explainable educational speech: What happened · Why it matters · What does **not** follow · What happens next (EIP-003 spirit; EL-010). |
| **Student interaction** | Post-session summary card / screen. Version 1 content (plain language): (1) Topic worked today; (2) Coverage effect — e.g. marked completed studying / still in progress; (3) Practice outcome recorded (or “no practice logged — estimates unchanged”); (4) Explicit non-claim: finishing the session is not mastery; (5) Next focus preview. Avoid defensive multi-disclaimer walls (Blind Review language fatigue). Prefer one honest paragraph + three short facts. |
| **Educational meaning** | Feedback is Guidance speech consuming state — **never** Evidence of understanding (Lifecycle §1.2 rule 4). |
| **Educational states affected** | Presentation only. Must not mutate Evidence, Estimates, or Study Progress beyond what Steps 4–6 already did. |
| **Evidence created** | None. |
| **What the student should feel** | Oriented and respected: “I understand what changed and what didn’t. I know what tomorrow asks of me.” |

---

### Step 8 — Tomorrow’s Guidance

| Field | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Bridge today into the next authorised commitment so the student has a reason to return tomorrow (EL-002, EL-003, EL-008 as advisory). |
| **Student interaction** | See tomorrow’s (or next session’s) Mission topic and short why; optionally see advisory notes that are **labelled advice**, not silent Learning Mode overrides. Dashboard strip updates consistently: one coverage story, no contradictory percentages. |
| **Educational meaning** | Under Version 1 Learning Mode, tomorrow’s Mission follows Current Learning Topic from updated Study Progress + syllabus order. Recommendations may advise review or extra practice using Evidence when available, but must **not** silently retarget Learning Mode mission focus (EL-008, EL-009; Constitution VI). |
| **Educational states affected** | Current Learning Topic reconciles after Study Progress updates. New Mission generated for next study day/session. Readiness / Estimated Knowledge narration may update only if Evidence succession occurred. Advisory Recommendation artefacts may refresh from new state — still advice. |
| **Evidence created** | None. Guidance consumes Evidence; Guidance never authors Evidence. |
| **What the student should feel** | Continuity and pull: “Tomorrow already knows where I left off — and if I practised, it remembers how I did.” |

**Generation rule (behavioural, not algorithmic):** Tomorrow’s Mission is regenerated from **updated educational state** (Study Progress + Current Learning Topic + plan context). It is not a random alternative topic, not Adaptive interruption, and not “intelligence” without Evidence.

## 5. Educational Flow

### 5.1 Happy path (Version 1 complete day)

```
[1] Open Today's Mission (Current Learning Topic under Learning Mode)
        ↓
[2] Study Activities (external or in-app material; engagement may be recorded)
        ↓
[3] Question Practice (structured results attributed to topic)  ← understanding spine opens
        ↓
[4] Session Completion (mission closes; Study Progress may advance)
        ↓
[5] Session Reflection (soft signals)
        ↓
[6] Educational Evidence produced (authorised Observations → Evidence)
        ↓  (existing Twin estimation paths may revise estimates when lawful)
[7] Student Feedback (What / Why / What does not follow / Next)
        ↓
[8] Tomorrow’s Mission generated from updated Current Learning Topic / state
```

### 5.2 Coverage-only path (lawful but thin)

Student completes Steps 1–2 and 4 without Step 3:

- Study Progress may advance.  
- Soft reflection (Step 5) may exist.  
- Understanding Evidence remains thin or absent.  
- Estimated Knowledge / Estimated Readiness must remain silent or unchanged — **honest understatement, not empty charts promising data that never arrives**.  
- Tomorrow’s Mission still advances on the coverage spine.  
- Feedback must say plainly: coverage moved; understanding estimates did not.

This path is constitutionally lawful and product-honest. It must **not** be the only path advertised as “adaptive readiness.”

### 5.3 State mutation map (Version 1)

| Step | Study Progress | Observation | Educational Evidence | Estimated Knowledge / Mastery | Current Learning / Next Mission | Readiness narration |
|------|----------------|-------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------------|---------------------|
| 1 Begin | — | Optional admin | — | — | Presented | — |
| 2 Study | Possibly later | EV-01 / EV-10 possible | Engagement only | Not alone | — | Pace soft only |
| 3 Practice | No alone | EV-03 / EV-04 | Yes (understanding) | May succeed via Twin later | — | May gain warrant later |
| 4 Complete | May advance | EV-02 admin | Not from checkbox | Never from completion alone | May advance via Study Progress | — |
| 5 Reflect | No alone | EV-08 / EV-09 | Soft only | Weak / never sole | — | Cautious soft |
| 6 Evidence | — | Authorised gate | Accumulates | Lawful succession only | — | Lawful factors only |
| 7 Feedback | — | — | — | Presentation | Preview | Presentation |
| 8 Tomorrow | Consumes | — | Consumes | Consumes if present | Regenerates Mission | Consumes if present |

### 5.4 Relationship to Mission task shapes (present product)

Blind Review observed three mission tasks (study minutes · practice minutes · reflect minutes). LXP-001 maps them as follows:

| Mission task shape (today) | LXP step | Required Version 1 product upgrade |
|----------------------------|----------|-------------------------------------|
| Focus / Core Reading minutes | Step 2 | Confirmable study engagement; truthful external-materials stance |
| Apply / further questions minutes | Step 3 | **Must add outcome capture** (attempted / correct or equivalent) |
| Reflect and review minutes | Step 5 | **Must add captured reflection**, not timer-only |
| Mark Session Complete | Step 4 + 6–8 | Completion remaining; then Evidence packet + Feedback + next Mission |

## 6. Minimum Legitimate Improvement Thresholds

This section answers when estimates and guidance may **legitimately improve**. It states educational thresholds, not algorithms.

### 6.1 Estimated Knowledge

| Question | Version 1 educational answer |
|----------|------------------------------|
| **Minimum interactions before it may improve** | At least one session producing **attributed structured practice outcomes** for a syllabus unit (Step 3 → EV-03/EV-04), authorised into Educational Evidence, then interpreted by existing Twin / estimation paths. |
| **Not enough alone** | Opening Mission; reading confirmation; mission completion checkbox; reflection; confidence; time spent; Study Progress declaration. |
| **Accumulation honesty** | One session may allow a **first provisional** estimate to appear or stir. Strong language and lasting mastery speech still require accumulation (Constitution V §5; Evidence Model Level rules). |
| **Student-facing consequence** | Until Step 3 data exists, surfaces must say understanding is not yet estimable — not show contradictory coverage proxies as if they were knowledge. |

### 6.2 Estimated Readiness

| Question | Version 1 educational answer |
|----------|------------------------------|
| **Minimum interactions before it may improve** | Readiness may incorporate **pace / coverage / plan-time factors** with thin history (cautious, labelled). Readiness that claims **knowledge-informed preparedness** requires the same understanding Evidence backbone as Estimated Knowledge — i.e. practice outcomes over time, not coverage percentage theatre. |
| **Not enough alone** | A single mission tick; three contradictory coverage %; empty analytics placeholders. |
| **Legitimate early signal** | “Pace matches plan” from Study Progress + time budget can be honest early. “You are becoming exam-ready in knowledge terms” cannot. |
| **Student-facing consequence** | Separate **plan readiness** (pace/coverage) from **knowledge readiness** (Evidence-backed) in speech so Blind Review-style distrust cannot recur. |

### 6.3 Educational Guidance (missions + recommendations)

| Guidance type | Minimum before it may *improve* (vs merely exist) |
|---------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| **Today’s / Tomorrow’s Mission (Learning Mode)** | Exists from Study Progress + syllabus day one. **Improves in student value** when Steps 3–7 close the loop so tomorrow’s commitment reflects both coverage advance *and* remembered practice/reflection. Topic selection rule itself remains Learning Mode sequential until Mode authority changes. |
| **Advisory recommendations** | May always advise “continue current topic.” **Material improvement** (personalised review / weakness honesty) requires authorised Evidence history for the student — typically multiple Step-3 outcomes across sessions, still advisory, still non-commandeering. |
| **Explainability** | Improves immediately when Feedback (Step 7) states What / Why / Next from real session data rather than boilerplate hedging. |

### 6.4 Minimum daily packet for a “complete” LXP day

To count as a **complete Version 1 learning day** (both spines touched):

1. Session started on an authorised Mission topic;  
2. Study activity acknowledged;  
3. **At least one structured practice outcome recorded** for that topic;  
4. Session completed;  
5. Brief reflection captured **or** explicitly skipped with honest consequence;  
6. Feedback shown;  
7. Next Mission available from updated state.

Coverage-only days remain allowed; they are not complete LXP days for understanding claims.

## 7. The Daily-Use Answer

### 7.1 Most important question

> What is the minimum Version 1 experience that would make the Blind Review student say:  
> **“Now I understand why I should open Kwalitec every day.”**

### 7.2 Answer

The minimum Version 1 experience is **not** an in-app Core Reading corpus, not Adaptive Mode, and not Digital Twin theatre.

It is a **closed daily coach loop**:

1. **One decision** — Today’s Mission tells me the next syllabus topic and why (sequence continuity).  
2. **One measured practice habit** — I log (or complete) today’s question outcomes for that topic in less than two minutes.  
3. **One honest close** — After I finish, Kwalitec tells me what coverage moved, what practice was recorded, and explicitly does **not** claim mastery from a tick.  
4. **One reason to return** — Tomorrow’s Mission is waiting, and if I practised, the product’s guidance and analytics are no longer permanently empty — they move only when Evidence warrants, and they **agree with themselves**.

That is sufficient for the Blind Review student’s daily-use sentence because it supplies the three missing assets called out in the review’s bottom line:

| Blind Review need | LXP-001 minimum response |
|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Something to actually study *(or a truthful study path)* | Named study step + external-materials honesty; optional content later |
| A way to record how I did | Step 3 structured practice capture |
| Numbers I can believe | Single coverage spine + Evidence-backed estimates only when earned; no contradictory % theatre |

**Emotional test:** After one real week, the student should feel: *“If I skip opening Kwalitec, I lose continuity of plan and the memory of how practice went — not just a checklist.”*

Anything less (Mission + green ticks alone) fails the Blind Review again.

### 8.1 In scope (design commitments for subsequent implementation)

| Full eight-step learning loop as product behaviour | Steps 1–8 |

### 8.2 Explicitly out of scope for LXP-001 Version 1

| Item | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Inventing estimation / readiness algorithms | Forbidden by capability charter; Twin paths already owned elsewhere |
| Inventing AI tutoring or generative content | Forbidden; Constitution II §1.3, §1.10 |
| Inventing recommendation mathematics | Forbidden; EL-008 behaviour already governed |
| Digital Twin redesign | Forbidden; Twin boundary remains EL-012 |
| Full in-app question bank / Core Reading corpus | Desirable product later; **not** the minimum daily-use answer |
| Revision / Diagnostic / Adaptive Mode mission commandeering | Constitution Article VI — future authorised modes |
| Mock exams / official exam Evidence pathways as required daily loop | Future Evidence Authority expansion |
| Curriculum breadth beyond working subjects | Separate product integrity work (Blind Review subject hollow-shell issue) |
| Application code in this capability | Design only — Architecture Review next |

### 8.3 Dependencies on already-certified educational assets

LXP-001 consumes, does not redefine:

- Study Progress, Current Learning Topic, Mission, Completion (Constitution IV; EL-001–004)
