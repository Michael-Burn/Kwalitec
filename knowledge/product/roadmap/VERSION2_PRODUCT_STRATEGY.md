# VERSION2_PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md

## Status

POST-RC2

## Purpose

This document defines the strategic direction of Version 2.

It is **not** a backlog.

It is **not** an implementation plan.

It is the decision framework used to determine what deserves to become Version 2.

---

# Guiding Principle

Version 2 will be driven by evidence.

Ideas do not become features.

Evidence creates features.

---

# Sources of Evidence

Only the following may justify Version 2 work.

## 1. Internal Alpha

Observed student behaviour.

Examples:

- repeated hesitation
- abandoned workflows
- revision patterns
- engagement trends

---

## 2. Founder Operational Health

Operational metrics.

Examples:

- inactive learners
- repeated failures
- revision adoption
- mission completion
- engagement decline

---

## 3. Feedback

Repeated themes.

Not individual requests.

A suggestion should appear multiple times before entering research.

---

## 4. Educational Research

Evidence from learning science.

Professional education.

Cognitive psychology.

Assessment theory.

---

## 5. Vision Journal

Founder ideas.

Ideas begin here.

They do not automatically become implementation work.

---

# Feature Lifecycle

Every capability follows the same lifecycle.

Vision

Research

↓

Validation

↓

Architecture

↓

Implementation

↓

Release

↓

Operational Observation

↓

Iteration

---

# Version 2 Mission

Transform Kwalitec from:

A structured study platform

into

An evidence-driven educational intelligence platform.

---

# Strategic Objectives

## Objective 1

Student Digital Twin

Understand how each learner studies.

Not just what they complete.

---

## Objective 2

Educational Intelligence

Move beyond deterministic recommendations.

Introduce explainable reasoning.

## Objective 3

Decision Engine

next topic

practice mix

session length

study priorities

using explainable evidence.

## Objective 4

Founder Intelligence

## Objective 5

Learning Evidence Graph

sessions

practice

missions

revision

feedback

engagement

---

# Version 2 Success Measures

Version 2 succeeds if it demonstrably improves:

Student consistency

Revision quality

Study efficiency

Founder visibility

Educational outcomes

Every improvement must be measurable.

---

# Explicit Non-Goals

Version 2 is not:

A chatbot

A generic AI wrapper

A collection of LLM prompts

A feature race

A redesign of Version 1

Every capability must strengthen educational decision-making.

---

# Release Philosophy

Version 2 should be delivered as a sequence of independently valuable milestones.

Each milestone must improve the product even if development stopped immediately afterwards.

---

# Founder Rule

Never implement a feature because it sounds exciting.

Implement it because evidence demonstrates educational value.

---

# Final Principle

Kwalitec exists to help students make better learning decisions.

Every Version 2 capability should improve the quality of those decisions while remaining transparent, explainable, and grounded in educational evidence.
