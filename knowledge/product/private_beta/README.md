## Global constraints

- No public launch.
- No public self-service registration.
- Invite-only accounts (admin / `create-admin` / controlled provisioning).
- EOS canonical runtime; do not reintroduce dual educational brains for beta.
- Align communications with Vision 2030 (learning, not activity; explainable guidance).
