# Private Beta — Release Notes Policy

**Status:** Process prepared

## Goal

Beta participants always know what changed, what is intentional, and what is not yet claimed.

## Rules

1. Every private beta deploy that changes learner behaviour gets release notes.
2. Lead with student-facing outcomes (clarity, guidance, reliability) — not internal epic codes alone.
3. Never claim guaranteed pass rates or unverified readiness.
4. Call out known limitations honestly (Vision / Blueprint product promise).
5. Link to prior notes; keep a short changelog under `docs/release/` or beta folder as chosen by release operator.
