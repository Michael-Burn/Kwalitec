# Private Beta — Onboarding

**Status:** Process prepared (no new educational feature work)

## Goal

Every beta participant knows what Kwalitec is for, what to do first, and how to give useful feedback — without feeling lost or overwhelmed (Vision experience principles).

## Recommended flow (ops)

1. **Invite** — Founder issues account (no public registration).
2. **Welcome note** — Purpose: intelligent study companion; north star is learning toward pass probability; not a question bank or gamification app.
3. **First session** — Complete onboarding / calibration already in product; start Today's Session.
4. **Orientation checklist** — Home (what now), Journey (progress), History (evidence), Revision, Reflection when prompted.
5. **Expectations** — Bugs expected; educational honesty preferred over polish; feedback channels linked.

## Artefacts to reuse

- Existing onboarding surfaces (`/student` journey, alpha onboarding where still applicable)
- Product Language Guide for copy
- Internal Alpha Playbook habits for dogfooding

## Out of scope

- Redesigning onboarding UX
- Public marketing site launch
- Guaranteeing exam pass in welcome copy (Blueprint product promise)
